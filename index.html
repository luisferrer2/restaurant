Our story

Rooted in Flavor

La Palma started as a family recipe passed down through three generations. What began as Sunday lunches in Barranquilla became a restaurant built on the belief that food should taste like home.

Every dish we make is prepared fresh daily — no shortcuts, no frozen ingredients. Just real Caribbean cooking with real love.
